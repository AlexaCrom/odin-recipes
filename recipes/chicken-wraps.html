Chicken Wraps

Home

Description

These healthy chicken wraps are the perfect meal for those wanting something, healthy, tasty and easy!

Ingredients

  • 4 Tortillas
  • 400g of chicken
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cornflour
  • Garlic powder
  • Paprika seasoning
  • Oil
  • Spicy Mayo

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken into strips
  2. Mix the cornflour and seasonings into a bowl
  3. Dredge the cut chicken in the cornflour
  4. Place the chicken in hot oil
  5. Cook on both sides for 6-8 minutes
  6. Dress the wraps with lettuce, tomato, spicy mayo and the fried chicken strips
  7. Enjoy!
