Noodle Soup

Home

Description

A bowl of warm hearty noodle soup, perfect for days when under the weather!

Ingredients

  • 400g chicken
  • Garlic cloves
  • Ginger
  • Soy sauce
  • Egg noodles
  • Chicken stock

Steps

  1. Cook the chicken in a pan and then leave to the side
  2. In the same pan, add 2l of water and extra chicken stock
  3. Add garlic, ginger, soy sauce and bring to a boil
  4. Let simmer until reduced to half
  5. Add egg noodles and cook through
  6. Serve in a bowl with the chicken cooked before
  7. Enjoy!
