Beef Rice Bowl

Home

Description

A healthy bowl of beef, rice and vegetables, perfect dish to warm you up!

Ingredients

  • 400g beef
  • 1 cup of rice
  • Brocolli
  • Soy sauce
  • Butter
  • Onion
  • Beef stock
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Thinly slice your beef
  2. Drizzle oil in a pain, add onions and cook until soft
  3. Put rice in rice cooker
  4. Add beef to the frying pain with onions and cook beef through
  5. Once cooked, add soy sauce, beef stock and black pepper
  6. When the sauce has thickened, serve over cooked rice and with steamed brocolli
  7. Enjoy!
